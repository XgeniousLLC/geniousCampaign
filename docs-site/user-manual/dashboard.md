## Dashboard

What this page is for: a 30-day, at-a-glance view of how your sending is performing — totals, trend, recent campaigns, and recent opens/clicks.

**Where to find it:** click **Dashboard** at the top of the sidebar, or it's simply the home page (`/`).

![Dashboard](../assets/screenshots/dashboard.png)

### What you'll see

1. **Stat cards** — Sent, Open rate, Click rate, and Bounce rate, each covering the last 30 days, with a supporting count underneath (e.g. total send attempts, number of opens).
2. **Engagement over time** — a line chart of opens (indigo) vs. clicks (green) per day across the last 30 days, so you can spot a spike or a drop-off at a glance.
3. **Recent campaigns** — the 5 most recently created campaigns with their sent/open/click counts and status badge. Click a campaign name to jump to its detail page, or **View all** to go to the full Campaigns list.
4. **Recent activity** — the last 10 open/click events across all campaigns and sequences, newest first, each showing which campaign it belongs to and a timestamp.

### Things to know

- Everything on this page is read-only — there's nothing to configure here, only to read.
- The window is fixed at 30 days; there's no date-range picker on this page today.
- "Recent activity" shows opens and clicks only (not sends, bounces, or unsubscribes) — check **Email Log** for the complete per-send history.
- Numbers refresh when you load or revisit the page — they don't live-update while you're looking at them.
